About ST Sportz

Coimbatore's Premier Indoor Sports Facility

Our Story

ST Sportz was founded with a vision to provide Coimbatore with a world-class indoor sports facility that promotes fitness, skill development, and community building through sports.

What started as a dream to create the best badminton facility in the region has now evolved into a comprehensive sports center offering both badminton and pickleball courts, along with professional training programs.

Our facility is designed to cater to players of all skill levels, from beginners learning the basics to advanced players preparing for tournaments. We believe that sports have the power to transform lives and build stronger communities.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide world-class sports facilities and training programs that inspire excellence, promote fitness, and build a vibrant sports community in Coimbatore.

Our Vision

To become the leading sports facility in South India, recognized for excellence in infrastructure, training, and community engagement.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Community, Innovation, and Sportsmanship guide everything we do at ST Sportz.
