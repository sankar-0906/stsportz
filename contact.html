Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a court?

You can book a court by calling us at +91 89258 93094, sending us an enquiry through the form above, or visiting our facility directly.

What are your operating hours?

We are open daily from 5 AM to 11 PM, including weekends and holidays.

Do you provide equipment?

Yes, we provide basic equipment for rent. You can also bring your own equipment if preferred.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have parking for 15 cars and 40 bikes with secure storage facilities.

Can I book for events?

Yes, we host corporate events and family outings. Contact us for customized packages and pricing.

Do you offer coaching?

Yes, we offer professional coaching for both badminton and pickleball through our partnership with Padukone Sports Management.
