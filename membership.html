Membership Terms & Conditions

Age Requirement

Members must be 12 years and above. Children under 12 must be accompanied by adults.

Renewal Policy

Membership renewals should happen on time to avoid losing your reserved slot. We have limited member capacity.

Booking Policy

Bookings are subject to availability. Members get priority booking over non-members.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation will attract charges. Please inform us at least 24 hours in advance.

Dress Code

Only permitted shoe types are allowed. Proper sports attire is recommended.

Safety & Liability

We provide parking and storage facilities, but we don't take responsibility for thefts. Please secure your belongings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my membership plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your membership plan at the beginning of each month.

What if I miss a session?

Missed sessions cannot be carried forward. We recommend booking your slots in advance.

Can I share my membership?

No, memberships are non-transferable and can only be used by the registered member.

Do you offer family memberships?

Currently, we offer individual memberships. Family members can book separate memberships.

What equipment is provided?

Basic equipment is available for rent. Members can also bring their own equipment.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership by giving us 30 days written notice. No refunds for partial months.
