Usage Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. General Rules

All users of ST Sportz facilities must adhere to the following general rules:

  • Respect all facility equipment and property
  • Maintain cleanliness and hygiene
  • Follow safety guidelines and instructions
  • Be respectful to staff and other users
  • No smoking or consumption of alcohol on premises
  • No pets allowed (except service animals)

2. Court Booking and Usage

2.1 Booking Rules

  • Advance booking is required for all court usage
  • Bookings can be made up to 7 days in advance
  • Maximum booking duration: 2 hours per session
  • Cancellation must be made at least 2 hours before the scheduled time
  • No-show bookings will be charged full rate

2.2 Court Usage

  • Arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled time
  • Wear appropriate sports shoes (non-marking soles)
  • Use only approved equipment
  • Keep noise levels reasonable
  • Clean up after your session

3. Membership Rules

  • Membership is non-transferable
  • Membership fees are non-refundable
  • Members must carry their membership card
  • Membership benefits cannot be shared
  • Violation of rules may result in membership suspension

4. Safety and Health

  • Users participate at their own risk
  • Report any injuries or accidents immediately
  • Follow all posted safety instructions
  • Use protective equipment when recommended
  • Stay hydrated during physical activity
  • Do not use facilities if feeling unwell

5. Equipment Usage

  • Equipment is provided on a first-come, first-served basis
  • Handle all equipment with care
  • Report damaged equipment immediately
  • Return equipment to designated areas after use
  • Personal equipment is allowed but must meet safety standards

6. Dress Code

  • Wear appropriate sports attire
  • Non-marking sports shoes are mandatory
  • No jeans, formal wear, or inappropriate clothing
  • Shirts must be worn at all times
  • Follow sport-specific dress requirements

7. Facility Access

  • Access is granted only during operating hours
  • Valid booking or membership required for entry
  • Guests must be accompanied by members
  • Maximum 4 guests per member
  • Guests must follow all facility rules

8. Prohibited Activities

  • Smoking or use of tobacco products
  • Consumption of alcohol or drugs
  • Gambling or betting activities
  • Commercial photography without permission
  • Unauthorized coaching or training
  • Disruptive or aggressive behavior
  • Use of facilities for non-sports activities

9. Photography and Recording

  • Personal photography is allowed for personal use only
  • Commercial photography requires prior permission
  • Respect others' privacy when taking photos
  • No recording of other users without consent
  • Staff may take photos for facility promotion

10. Parking and Transportation

  • Free parking is available for users
  • Park in designated areas only
  • Secure your vehicles and belongings
  • ST Sportz is not responsible for vehicle damage or theft
  • Follow traffic rules in the parking area

11. Food and Beverages

  • Water bottles are allowed on courts
  • Food consumption is restricted to designated areas
  • No food or beverages on the courts
  • Use vending machines for refreshments
  • Clean up after consuming food or beverages

12. Emergency Procedures

  • In case of emergency, contact staff immediately
  • Follow evacuation procedures if required
  • First aid kit is available at reception
  • Emergency contact numbers are posted
  • Stay calm and follow staff instructions

13. Violations and Penalties

  • First violation: Warning
  • Second violation: Temporary suspension (1 week)
  • Third violation: Extended suspension (1 month)
  • Serious violations: Permanent ban
  • Damage to property: Full cost recovery

14. Changes to Policy

ST Sportz reserves the right to modify this usage policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes through:

  • Website updates
  • Email notifications (for members)
  • Posted notices at the facility
  • Social media announcements
